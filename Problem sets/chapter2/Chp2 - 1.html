Determine if the columns of the given matrix span R2

$$\begin{bmatrix} 15 & -6 \\ -5 & 2 \end{bmatrix}$$







In order to span the R2, there should be at least two linearly independent column vectors in the matrix

1. Checking if one column vector is the multiple of another vector since only two columns vectors in the matrix.

$$(-\frac{5}{2}) \begin{bmatrix} -6 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 15 \\ -5 \end{bmatrix}$$

Since two column vectors are linearly dependent, so the columns of the given matrix can't span R2
