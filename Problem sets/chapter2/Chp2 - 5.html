Determine if the columns of the given matrix span R3

$$\begin{bmatrix} 3 & 1 & 0 \\ 5 & -2 & -1 \\ 4 & -4 & -3 \\ \end{bmatrix}$$







In order to span the R3, there should be at least three linearly independent column vectors in the matrix

1. Applying Elementary Row Operations to get the REF: $$\xrightarrow[3R_3 - 4R_1 \rightarrow R_3]{3R_2 - 5R_1 \rightarrow R_2}$$ $$\begin{bmatrix} 3 & 1 & 0 \\ \textcolor{orange}{0} & \textcolor{orange}{-11} & \textcolor{orange}{-3} \\ \textcolor{orange}{0} & \textcolor{orange}{-16} & \textcolor{orange}{-9} \\ \end{bmatrix}$$

By looking last two rows, we know that there must be three pivots in the matrix since last two rows are not the multiple of each other. Feel free to check it yourself!

The columns of the given matrix span R3
