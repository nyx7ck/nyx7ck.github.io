Find all values of h such that the vectors \(\{\ \vec{a_1}, \vec{a_2}, \vec{a_3} \}\) span R3, where $$\vec{a_1} = \begin{bmatrix} -1 \\ h \\ 7 \end{bmatrix}, \quad \vec{a_2} = \begin{bmatrix} 4 \\ -2 \\ 5 \end{bmatrix}, \quad \vec{a_3} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ -3 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix}$$







1. In order to span R3, the matrix made by these column vectors should have at least three pivots:

$$\begin{bmatrix} -1 & 4 & 1 \\ h & -2 & -3 \\ 7 & 5 & 2 \\ \end{bmatrix}$$

$$\xrightarrow[R_3 + 7R_1 \rightarrow R_3]{R_2 + hR_1 \rightarrow R_2}$$

$$\begin{bmatrix} 2 & h & 1 \\ 0 & 4h - 2 & h - 3 \\ 0 & 33 & 9 \\ \end{bmatrix}$$

$$\xrightarrow{ \frac{R_2}{4h - 2} \rightarrow R_2 }$$

$$\begin{bmatrix} 2 & h & 1 \\ 0 & 1 & \frac{h - 3}{4h - 2} \\ 0 & 33 & 9 \\ \end{bmatrix}$$

$$\xrightarrow{R_3 - 33R_2 \rightarrow R_3}$$

$$\begin{bmatrix} 2 & h & 1 \\ 0 & 1 & \frac{h - 3}{4h - 2} \\ 0 & 0 & 9 - 33 \frac{h - 3}{4h - 2} \\ \end{bmatrix}$$

2. Since we want three pivots, the following equation have to be satisfied:

\[ 9 - 33 \frac{h - 3}{4h - 2} \neq 0 \]


\[ 9 \neq 33 \frac{h - 3}{4h - 2} \]


\[ \frac{h - 3}{4h - 2} \neq \frac{9}{33} \]


\[ 36h - 18 \neq 33h - 99 \]


\[ 3h = - 81 \]


\[ h \neq -27 \]

Thus, \[ \{ h \in \mathbb{R} \quad |\quad h \neq -27 \} \]
