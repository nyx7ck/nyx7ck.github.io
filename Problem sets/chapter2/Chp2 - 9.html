Find all values of \( h \) such that the vectors \( \{ \vec{a_1}, \vec{a_2} \} \) span \(\mathbb{R}^2\), where $$\vec{a_1} = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 4 \end{bmatrix}, \quad \vec{a_2} = \begin{bmatrix} h \\ 6 \end{bmatrix}$$







In order to span \(\mathbb{R}^2\), two vectors could not be the multiple of each other, the following equation has to be satisfied: \[ \frac{2}{4} \neq \frac{h}{6} \]
\[ 4h \neq 12 \]
\[ h \neq 3 \] Thus, \[ \{ h \in \mathbb{R} \quad |\quad h \neq 3 \} \]
