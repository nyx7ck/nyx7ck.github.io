Determine the matrix below is in Row Echelon Form, Reduced Row Echelon Form or neither.

$$\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 3 & -2 \\ 0 & 2 & 6 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix}$$







First condition: checking if the zeros row(s) are in the bottom. \[\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 3 & -2 \\ 0 & 2 & 6 \\ \textcolor{orange}{0} & \textcolor{orange}{0} & \textcolor{orange}{0} \end{bmatrix}\]

Fist condition passed!

Second condition: Check if the pivot is all positioned correctly. \[\begin{bmatrix} \textcolor{orange}{1} & 3 & -2 \\ 0 & \textcolor{orange}{2} & 6 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix}\]

Second condition passed!

It is in REF!

Third Condition: Check if all pivots are 1 and any numbers in the same column are zeros $$\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 3 & -2 \\ 0 &\cancel{2} & 6 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix}$$

Third condition failed

Thus, this matrix is in Row Echelon Form, but not in Row Reduced Echelon Form.
