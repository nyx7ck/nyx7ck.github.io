Determine the matrix below is in Row Echelon Form, Reduced Row Echelon Form or neither.

$$\begin{bmatrix} 3 & -3 & 1 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & -2 & 4 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix}$$







First condition: checking if the zeros row(s) are in the bottom.

No need to check this condition since no zero row(s) in the bottom.

Fist condition passed!

Second condition: Check if the pivot is all position correctly. $$\begin{bmatrix} 3 & -3 & 1 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & -2 & 4 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & \cancel{1} & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix}$$

Second condition failed!

It is not in REF!

Thus, this matrix is neither in Row Echelon Form nor in Row Reduced Echelon Form.
