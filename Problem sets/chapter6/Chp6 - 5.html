Find the characteristic polynomial, the given eigenvalues, and a basis for each eigenspace for the matrix \(A\) \[ A = \begin{bmatrix} 2 & 5 & 1 \\ 0 & -3 & -1 \\ 2 & 14 & 4 \\ \end{bmatrix} \]






\[ \det(A - \lambda I) = \det(\begin{bmatrix} 2 - \lambda & 5 & 1 \\ 0 & -3 - \lambda & -1 \\ 2 & 14 & 4 - \lambda \\ \end{bmatrix}) \]
\[ \det(\begin{bmatrix} 2 - \lambda & 5 & 1 \\ 0 & -3 - \lambda & -1 \\ 2 & 14 & 4 - \lambda \\ \end{bmatrix}) = (2 - \lambda)(\lambda^2 - \lambda + 2) + (2)(\lambda - 2) \]
\[ (2 - \lambda)(\lambda^2 - \lambda + 2) + (2)(\lambda - 2) = (2 - \lambda)(\lambda^2 - \lambda) = \lambda(\lambda - 1)(2 - \lambda) \]

When \(\lambda = 0\) :
\[ A - \lambda I = \vec{0} \iff \left[\begin{array}{ccc | c} 2 - 0 & 5 & 1 & 0\\ 0 & -3 - 0 & -1 & 0\\ 2 & 14 & 4 - 0 & 0\\ \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{ccc | c} 2 & 5 & 1 & 0\\ 0 & -3 & -1 & 0\\ 2 & 14 & 4 & 0\\ \end{array}\right] \]
\[ \left[\begin{array}{ccc | c} 2 & 5 & 1 & 0\\ 0 & -3 & -1 & 0\\ 2 & 14 & 4 & 0\\ \end{array}\right] \xrightarrow{} \left[\begin{array}{ccc | c} 2 & 5 & 1 & 0\\ 0 & -3 & -1 & 0\\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0\\ \end{array}\right] \]
\[ \text{basis}\{\begin{bmatrix} \frac{1}{3} \\ -\frac{1}{3} \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}\} \]
When \(\lambda = 1\) :
\[ A - \lambda I = \vec{0} \iff \left[\begin{array}{ccc | c} 2 - 1 & 5 & 1 & 0\\ 0 & -3 - 1 & -1 & 0\\ 2 & 14 & 4 - 1 & 0\\ \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{ccc | c} 1 & 5 & 1 & 0\\ 0 & -4 & -1 & 0\\ 2 & 14 & 3 & 0\\ \end{array}\right] \]
\[ \left[\begin{array}{ccc | c} 1 & 5 & 1 & 0\\ 0 & -4 & -1 & 0\\ 2 & 14 & 3 & 0\\ \end{array}\right] \xrightarrow{} \left[\begin{array}{ccc | c} 1 & 5 & 1 & 0\\ 0 & -4 & -1 & 0\\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0\\ \end{array}\right] \]
\[\text{basis}\{\begin{bmatrix} \frac{1}{4} \\ -\frac{1}{4} \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}\}\]
When \(\lambda = 2\) : \[ A - \lambda I = \vec{0} \iff \left[\begin{array}{ccc | c} 2 - 2 & 5 & 1 & 0\\ 0 & -3 - 2 & -1 & 0\\ 2 & 14 & 4 - 2 & 0\\ \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{ccc | c} 0 & 5 & 1 & 0\\ 0 & -5 & -1 & 0\\ 2 & 14 & 2 & 0\\ \end{array}\right] \]
\[ \left[\begin{array}{ccc | c} 0 & 5 & 1 & 0\\ 0 & -5 & -1 & 0\\ 2 & 14 & 2 & 0\\ \end{array}\right] \xrightarrow{} \left[\begin{array}{ccc | c} 2 & 14 & 2 & 0\\ 0 & -5 & -1 & 0\\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0\\ \end{array}\right] \]
\[\text{basis}\{\begin{bmatrix} \frac{2}{5} \\ -\frac{1}{5} \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}\}\]
