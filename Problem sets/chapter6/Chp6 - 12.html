Row Operations do not change the eigenvalues of a matrix






Here is a counterexample:

\[ \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \text{ VS } \begin{bmatrix} 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 \end{bmatrix} \]

The matrix at the right is just multiply \(I_2\) by 2, but its eigenvalues change to 2 instead of 1.

Thus, the statement is False
