Find all values of h such that the vectors \(\{\ \vec{a_1}, \vec{a_2}, \vec{a_3} \}\) span R3, where $$\vec{a_1} = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 4 \\ 5 \end{bmatrix}, \quad \vec{a_2} = \begin{bmatrix} h \\ 8 \\ 10 \end{bmatrix}, \quad \vec{a_3} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 2 \\ 6 \end{bmatrix}$$







1. In order to span R3, the matrix made by these column vectors should have at least three pivots:

$$\begin{bmatrix} 2 & h & 1 \\ 4 & 8 & 2 \\ 5 & 10 & 6 \\ \end{bmatrix}$$

$$\xrightarrow[2R_3 - 5R_1 \rightarrow R_3]{R_2 - 2R_1 \rightarrow R_2}$$

$$\begin{bmatrix} 2 & h & 1 \\ 0 & 8 - 2h & 0 \\ 0 & 20 - 5h & 7 \\ \end{bmatrix}$$

$$\xrightarrow{R_3 - \frac{5}{2}R_2 \rightarrow R_3}$$

$$\begin{bmatrix} 2 & h & 1 \\ 0 & 8 - 2h & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 7 \\ \end{bmatrix}$$

2. Since we want three pivots, the following equation has to be satisfied:

\[ 8 - 2h \neq 0 \]


\[ 2h \neq 8 \]


\[ h \neq 4 \]

Thus, \[ \{ h \in \mathbb{R} \quad |\quad h \neq 4 \} \]
